Tokenomics Review Skill

Purpose

Analyze and improve Solana token designs to ensure sustainability, fairness, and long-term ecosystem alignment.

⸻

When to use

Use when user asks about:

* Token design
* Supply structure
* Vesting schedules
* Allocation breakdown
* Emissions strategy
* Token sustainability

⸻

Input Required

* Total supply
* Team allocation
* Investor allocation
* Community allocation
* Treasury allocation
* Vesting schedule (if available)

⸻

Analysis Framework

1. Supply Structure Health

Evaluate:

* Inflation risk
* Scarcity balance
* Distribution fairness

⸻

2. Allocation Breakdown

Benchmark healthy ranges:

* Community: 40–60%
* Team: 10–20%
* Investors: 10–25%
* Treasury: 10–20%

Flag:

* Over-centralization (>25% team)
* Weak community incentives (<35%)

⸻

3. Vesting Risk Analysis

Check:

* Early unlock pressure
* Dump risk timeline
* Misaligned incentives

⸻

4. Utility Assessment

Evaluate:

* Does token have real usage?
* Is it required for ecosystem participation?
* Is demand organic or forced?

⸻

5. Emissions Model

Identify:

* Inflation schedule
* Reward sustainability
* Long-term dilution impact

⸻

Output Format

Tokenomics Score (0–100)

Breakdown:

* Distribution fairness
* Incentive design
* Sustainability
* Utility strength

⸻

Red Flags

* Overallocated team supply
* No real token utility
* Unsustainable emissions
* Weak vesting controls

⸻

Recommendations

* Rebalance allocations
* Extend vesting
* Add utility loops
* Reduce inflation pressure

⸻

Final Verdict

* Strong / Moderate / High Risk
