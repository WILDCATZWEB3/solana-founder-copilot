Treasury Management Skill

Purpose

Help Solana founders manage project treasury for sustainability, runway optimization, and risk reduction.

⸻

When to use

Use when user asks about:

* Treasury allocation
* Burn rate
* Runway calculation
* Fund management
* Risk exposure
* Asset diversification

⸻

Input Required

* Treasury assets (SOL, USDC, tokens)
* Monthly burn rate
* Revenue (if any)
* Risk tolerance (low / medium / high)

⸻

Core Analysis

1. Runway Calculation

Formula:

Runway = Treasury / Monthly Burn

Output:

* Safe runway estimate
* Risk-adjusted runway

⸻

2. Asset Allocation Health

Check:

* % in stablecoins
* % in volatile assets (SOL, tokens)
* Liquidity availability

⸻

3. Risk Exposure

Identify:

* Overexposure to SOL volatility
* Lack of stable reserve
* Poor diversification

⸻

4. Sustainability Score

Evaluate:

* Burn efficiency
* Revenue generation
* Capital efficiency

⸻

Recommendations Engine

Always suggest:

* Minimum 6–12 months stable reserve
* Convert portion to stablecoins if overexposed
* Stake idle SOL where appropriate
* Reduce burn if runway < 6 months

⸻

Output Format

Treasury Health Score (0–100)

* Runway stability
* Risk exposure
* Efficiency
* Diversification

⸻

Key Insights

* Main risk
* Hidden inefficiency
* Survival probability

⸻

Action Plan (30 days)

1. Immediate risk fixes
2. Capital reallocation
3. Burn optimization
