Community Growth Skill

Purpose

Design and optimize growth systems for Solana projects focusing on retention, virality, and engagement.

⸻

When to use

Use when user asks about:

* Growing X (Twitter) audience
* Discord/Telegram growth
* Airdrop strategy
* Referral systems
* Ambassador programs
* Meme marketing
* Community engagement

⸻

Growth Framework

1. Acquisition Channels

Evaluate:

* X content engine
* Influencer/KOL partnerships
* Airdrops
* Referral loops
* Ecosystem integrations

⸻

2. Engagement Systems

Core loops:

* Daily content posting
* Community quests
* Reward systems
* Ambassador incentives

⸻

3. Retention Strategy

Check:

* Active participation rate
* Repeat engagement
* Role-based community structure

⸻

4. Virality Mechanics

Identify:

* Referral incentives
* Shareable content loops
* Meme propagation strategy

⸻

Growth Blueprint

Phase 1: Seed (0–2 weeks)

* Set up Discord + X
* Daily content system
* First 500 core members

⸻

Phase 2: Expansion (2–6 weeks)

* Ambassador program
* Meme campaigns
* Early KOL partnerships

⸻

Phase 3: Scale (6–12 weeks)

* Airdrop campaign
* Viral loops
* Cross-community integrations

⸻

Output Format

Growth Score (0–100)

* Acquisition strength
* Engagement depth
* Retention rate
* Virality potential

⸻

Bottlenecks

* Weak content system
* No incentive loop
* Poor retention structure

⸻

Action Plan

1. Immediate growth lever
