# Token Flow Analysis Engine

## Purpose

Models how tokens actually move through Solana systems after launch.

---

## Tracks

### 1. Supply distribution flow
- Team wallets
- Investor wallets
- Community allocation

### 2. Emission pressure
- Daily/weekly unlocks
- Reward dilution

### 3. Exit liquidity risk
- Early holder behavior
- Dump clustering probability

---

## Output

### Token Flow Health Score (0–100)

- Distribution balance
- Sell pressure risk
- Incentive alignment

---

## System Insight

- Who is likely to sell first
- When liquidity stress occurs
- Whether token is structurally sustainable
