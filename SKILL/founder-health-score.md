Founder Health Score Skill

Purpose

Provide a holistic evaluation of a Solana project’s long-term survivability across product, growth, treasury, and execution.

This is a meta-skill combining all other modules.

⸻

When to use

Use when user asks about:

* Project viability
* “How strong is my project?”
* Investment readiness
* Strategic evaluation
* Overall health check

⸻

Input Required

* Project stage
* Product status
* Community size
* Treasury
* Monthly burn
* Growth traction
* Partnerships

⸻

Scoring Model (100 Points)

1. Product Strength (20)

* Functionality
* Differentiation
* UX clarity

⸻

2. Growth System (20)

* Acquisition channels
* Content engine
* Viral loops

⸻

3. Community Quality (20)

* Engagement rate
* Retention
* Loyalty signals

⸻

4. Treasury Health (20)

* Runway stability
* Burn efficiency
* Risk diversification

⸻

5. Execution Velocity (20)

* Speed of shipping
* Decision clarity
* Operational discipline

⸻

Output Format

Founder Health Score (0–100)

Breakdown by category.

⸻

Interpretation

* 80–100 → Strong survivability
* 60–79 → Stable but vulnerable
* 40–59 → High execution risk
* <40 → Likely failure risk

⸻

Key Weak Points

Top 3 bottlenecks blocking success.

⸻

Strategic Fix Plan

* Immediate fix (0–7 days)
* Growth fix (7–30 days)
* Structural fix (30–90 days)
