# Liquidity Modeling Engine

## Purpose

Simulates how liquidity behaves under real trading conditions.

---

## Evaluates

### 1. LP depth stability
- Can pool absorb early selling?

### 2. Price impact sensitivity
- Slippage under sell pressure

### 3. LP incentive structure
- Are LPs rewarded to stay?

---

## Output

### Liquidity Risk Score (0–100)

- Depth stability
- Volatility tolerance
- Exit resistance

---

## System Output

- Breakdown point of LP
- Expected volatility spikes
- Recommended liquidity structure
