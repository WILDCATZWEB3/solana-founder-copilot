# Market Reality Check Engine

## Purpose

Evaluates whether a Solana project can survive real market conditions post-launch.

---

## What it analyzes

### 1. Liquidity survival risk
- Will LP hold under pressure?
- Early dump probability

### 2. Token shock analysis
- Supply unlock impact
- Insider distribution pressure

### 3. Demand reality
- Organic vs artificial demand
- Incentive-driven users vs real users

### 4. Market timing
- Cycle sensitivity
- Narrative saturation

---

## Output

### Market Viability Score (0–100)

- Liquidity stability
- Demand strength
- Supply pressure
- Timing fit

---

## System Output Format

- Survival probability (30/60/90 days)
- Primary failure vector
- Liquidity collapse risk (low/medium/high)
- Required mitigation actions
