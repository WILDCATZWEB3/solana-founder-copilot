Solana Founder Copilot

Production-grade AI skill for Solana founders.

Solana Founder Copilot helps founders plan launches, review tokenomics, manage treasury, prepare fundraising, build partnerships, grow communities, and evaluate launch readiness.

Problem

Most AI skills in crypto focus on code generation.

Most projects fail because of:

* Poor launch planning
* Weak distribution
* Lack of partnerships
* Unsustainable tokenomics
* Treasury mismanagement
* Poor fundraising preparation

Founder Copilot solves these operational problems.

Features

Launch Planning

Generate 30, 60, and 90-day execution roadmaps.

Tokenomics Advisor

Review token supply, allocations, vesting schedules, emissions, and incentives.

Treasury Manager

Evaluate runway, burn rate, diversification, and treasury risk.

Community Growth

Design growth systems for X, Discord, Telegram, ambassador programs, and referrals.

Fundraising Copilot

Prepare investor narratives, KPI frameworks, and fundraising strategies.

Partnership Copilot

Identify ecosystem opportunities and strategic partnerships.

Launch Readiness Auditor

Assess launch readiness across product, community, treasury, growth, and partnerships.

Founder Health Score

Generate a comprehensive score and action plan for project success.
